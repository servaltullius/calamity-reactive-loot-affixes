Build output `CalamityAffixes.dll` goes here:

  Data/SKSE/Plugins/CalamityAffixes.dll

Note:
- This repo may include `CalamityAffixes.dll.disabled` to avoid accidentally loading an out-of-date DLL.
- Replace it with your freshly built `CalamityAffixes.dll`.

Tip (MO2):
- If you build to a separate folder, copy the DLL into your MO2 mod's `SKSE/Plugins/` directory.
